Forum is now ready!

Our forum is now ready to be used. Although ready, it is a BETA version.

Send us your feedback or if you found any bugs to scscsarawak@gmail.com

Post any questions you like according to the rules and regulation in the forum.

Read the rules and regulation before joining.

Things to take note:

The button open shows that the discussion is still ongoing and you can still leave a comemnt on it.

The discussion will show a closed when the author of the discussion selected the best answer.

Click to follow the discussion, you will also receive an email when there is a comment added.

Due to storage limitation, the discussion with best answer or unactive discussion will be removed after one month, so save your answer before that.

You can find all the unit names for discussion under Channels > Units

Each individual will be given trophies based on reply and best answer.

  • 1 reply = 5 trophies
  • 1 best answer = 10 trophies

User who reaches 500 trophies can apply to become the moderator of the forum or choose not to. User who reaches 800 trophies can apply to be the admin for the forum.

Read the Markdown guideline to post in style.
